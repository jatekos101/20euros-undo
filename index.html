Join the coins until you reach 20 Euro! You can undo your moves with Z!

Continue Retry

HOW TO PLAY: Use your arrow keys to move the coins. If two or three mergeable coins touch, they merge into one! Your goal is to reach the 20 Euro note! Undo your last move with Z. You can undo as many moves as you want, but you can't undo after Game Over!

Mergeable combinations:
1+1=2
2+2+1=5
5+5=10
10+10=20 or 5+10+5=20
20+20+10=50
and so on.
